Magic Snake

Introduction of Magic Snake.

You can play Magic Snake inside a fixed sphere. Attempts of Snake growing without solving the collision will cost your lives. See how far you can get before you lose all your lives.

In hard mode, you only have 3 seconds to think about which direction to rotate, and no longer have chance to REGRET!!, Good luck!!

Try to earn score as much as you can, and unlock next levels when you reach certain points.

This is playing with classical 24 blocks Rubik's snake

See ranks for All Time Leader is not supported
